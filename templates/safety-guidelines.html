Marketplace Safety Guidelines

These guidelines help keep our student marketplace safe, fair, and trustworthy for buyers and sellers.

Account Safety
  • Never share your account password with anyone.
  • Always log out when using shared or public computers.
  • Use a strong password to protect your account.
Secure Payments
  • Always complete transactions through the marketplace platform.
  • Do not send money directly to sellers outside the system.
  • Check product details and seller ratings before purchasing.
Product Safety
  • Ensure that items listed are genuine and accurately described.
  • Do not list prohibited or illegal items.
  • Provide clear images and correct pricing.
Dispute Handling
  • If an issue occurs, raise a dispute through the platform.
  • Provide proof such as images or order details.
  • The platform team will review and resolve disputes fairly.
Community Conduct
  • Be respectful when communicating with buyers or sellers.
  • Do not post misleading or fraudulent listings.
  • Follow all marketplace rules and local laws.

By using this marketplace, you agree to follow these safety guidelines to ensure a secure and trustworthy environment for everyone.
